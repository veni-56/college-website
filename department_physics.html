About the Department

The Department of Physics was established in 1990, initially offering Physics as an ancillary subject. It was upgraded to a major department in 2003. The department is equipped with advanced laboratories and has produced university rank holders. It also focuses on practical knowledge like medical instrumentation, electrical appliances, and mobile communication to make students industry ready.

Vision

The Department aspires to provide the best scientific methods in teaching the basic principles of Physics, maintaining high standards in education and promoting applications in various technologies.

Mission

  • To provide high-quality Physics education and equip students for higher education.
  • To develop experimental skills and enable students to make instruments independently.
  • To relate physics concepts to social needs and competitive examinations.
