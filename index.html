About Our College

A.K.D. Dharma Raja Women's College is a renowned Arts & Science institution in Southern India and is affiliated to Madurai Kamaraj University. Established in 1990, to gratify the long-felt educational need of the women of Rajapalaiyam and neighboring community. It is administered by “Sri Rao Bahadur A.K.D.Dharma Raja Education Charity Trust, Rajapalaiyam” one of the oldest pioneer Educational Charity Trust in Virudhunagar District which was established in 1932. Our college campus is located amidst the green serenity of the Western Ghats, at Shenbagathoppu Road, Rajapalaiyam.
