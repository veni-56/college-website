The Women Development Cell (WDC) of our college is committed to addressing and advancing matters related to women‚Äôs development, welfare, and empowerment. The cell focuses on fostering a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for all women students and staff on campus.

Its core initiatives involve promoting gender equality, skill-building, awareness campaigns, and support systems that enhance women's academic and personal growth.

The WDC also plays a proactive role in ensuring women's health, hygiene, and safety, while working to build confidence and leadership among female students.

Vision

To create a gender-sensitive campus environment where women are empowered, respected, and equipped to achieve their full potential in all spheres of life.

Mission

  • To empower women through awareness, education, and self-development.
  • To encourage gender equality and prevent discrimination or harassment.
  • To organize workshops, seminars, and training programs focused on life skills, legal rights, and career development.
  • To provide a platform for support and counseling for women in need.
  • To build leadership qualities and promote active participation in academic and co-curricular activities.

Activities

The Women Development Cell organizes various activities throughout the academic year to promote awareness, empowerment, and personal development. Some key initiatives include:

  • Self-defense workshops and training
  • Legal awareness sessions
  • Health and hygiene awareness drives
  • Career counseling and soft skills training
  • Women‚Äôs Day celebrations and competitions
