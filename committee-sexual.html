Vision

To foster a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment where every individual is free from the threat of sexual harassment, and where dignity, equality, and justice prevail at all levels.

Mission

To prevent and address incidents of sexual harassment through awareness, education, and effective redressal mechanisms.

To empower individuals to report harassment without fear of retaliation.

To ensure a transparent, impartial, and timely resolution process in accordance with legal and ethical standards.

To promote a culture of zero tolerance towards sexual misconduct and uphold the fundamental right to safety and respect for all.

Objectives

To help women to realize their rights of freedom.

To ensure equal access of all facilities and participated in activities of the college.

To create a secure physical and social environment which will deter acts of sexual harassment.

Outcomes

1. Safe and Respectful Environment

2. Increased Awareness and Sensitization

3. Effective Complaint Redressal Mechanism

4. Empowered Community

5. Policy Implementation and Compliance

6. Reduction in Incidents
