About the Department

The Department of Computer Science was established in 1990. It currently has 4 faculty members and over 110 students. The department is well-equipped with modern laboratories and provides quality education, focusing on activity-based learning, industry exposure, and career readiness for students.

Vision

To empower students to reach the zenith by offering quality education and producing excellent computer professionals with strong ethical values and industry-ready skills.

Mission

  • To provide quality education at affordable cost to students from rural and backward areas.
  • To create a learning environment that supports lifelong learning and personal growth.
  • To promote industry-institute interaction and enhance employability through internships, workshops, and project-based learning.
  • To inculcate a sense of duty, discipline, responsibility, and patriotism among students.
