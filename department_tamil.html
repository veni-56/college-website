Vision

To nurture a community of young women who are proficient in Tamil language and literature, and who embody the rich cultural heritage of Tamil Nadu. We aim to produce leaders, poets, and thinkers who will contribute to the preservation and promotion of Tamil culture.

Mission

  • To impart knowledge of Tamil language and literature that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and communication.
  • To promote cultural awareness and appreciation among students through programs and activities.
  • To nurture leadership qualities, making students confident, articulate, and socially responsible.
  • To encourage creative writing, public speaking, and performance for skill development.
